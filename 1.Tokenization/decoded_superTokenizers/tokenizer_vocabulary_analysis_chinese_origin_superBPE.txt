MIXED_WITH_CHINESE - Tokens containing Chinese chars mixed with other content

OTHER_JAPANESE     - Japanese characters (Hiragana/Katakana)
OTHER_KOREAN       - Korean characters (Hangul: U+AC00-U+D7AF)
OTHER_RARE_CHINESE_UTF8 - Rare Chinese chars or UTF-8 byte fragments

OTHER_UNKNOWN      - Other unclassified tokens
