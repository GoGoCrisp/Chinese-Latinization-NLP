CATEGORY EXPLANATIONS

LATIN_LETTER       - Pure Latin letters (a-zA-Z)
LATIN_DIGIT        - Latin letters and digits combined
DIGIT              - Pure digits (0-9)
PUNCTUATION        - Punctuation marks and symbols

CHINESE_NCHAR      - N pure Chinese characters (Standard CJK: U+4E00-U+9FFF)
PINYIN_NCHAR       - N valid pinyin syllables (Space separated in token)
PINYIN_WITH_DIGIT  - Combination of valid pinyin syllables and digits
MIXED_WITH_CHINESE - Tokens containing Chinese chars mixed with other content

OTHER_JAPANESE     - Japanese characters (Hiragana/Katakana)
OTHER_KOREAN       - Korean characters (Hangul: U+AC00-U+D7AF)
OTHER_RARE_CHINESE_UTF8 - Rare Chinese chars or UTF-8 byte fragments
                     (CJK Extension: U+3400-U+4DBF, or byte notation <0xXX>)
OTHER_UNKNOWN      - Other unclassified tokens
